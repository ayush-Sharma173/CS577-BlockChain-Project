About Our Platform

We use blockchain technology to create a decentralized, transparent, and trustless environment for charitable donations. By leveraging Ethereum smart contracts, we guarantee that your donations are securely handled and verifiably tracked.

How it Works

Step 1: Choose a Campaign

Select a verified charity campaign from our list of causes.

Step 2: Make a Donation

Donate securely using Ethereum through our platform, directly from your wallet.

Step 3: Track Donations

View the progress of your donation in real-time, ensuring your contribution is utilized correctly.

Step 4: Smart Contract Disbursal

Once the donation target is met, smart contracts automatically release funds to the charity.
