We work in the hardest-to-reach places, where it’s toughest to be a child.

When you give to Save the Children, 85% of every donation goes straight to our mission to provide lifesaving relief to children.

  • ₹5000 can provide enough food to keep 3 children from going hungry for a month
  • ₹8000 can wrap 30 warm, cozy blankets around children affected by conflict
  • ₹10000 can provide 150 face masks to refugee health workers on the front lines

With your support, we can continue our work to keep children in the India and around the world healthy, educated and safe.
